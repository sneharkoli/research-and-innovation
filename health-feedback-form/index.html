Privacy Policy

This health feedback form collects anonymous health information for research and statistical analysis purposes.

  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • All responses are stored locally in your browser
  • Data is used only for demographic trend analysis
  • You can clear your data at any time

Data Usage

Your health information is used to:

  • Generate demographic health trend statistics
  • Create visualizations showing health patterns by age, gender, and location
  • Identify areas where health education might be beneficial
  • Support public health research initiatives

All data is aggregated and anonymized before analysis.
